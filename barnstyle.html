How Barn Style has been adapted

The use of rustic materials in an intentional style began with the High Tech movement in the 1970s. Designers started to use industrial and agricultural materials where they had never been used before: indoors. Galvanized troughs were used as bathtubs, corrugated roofing material was used as wainscoating and exterior building materials like barn boards or brick walls were used to decorative effect. Salvage has become a big business, and milling down old timber frames to use as floors in new construction is more popular than ever.
