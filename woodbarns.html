Wooden Barns

Wood barns are constructed using a timber frame: large timbers connected with mortise and tennon joinery and pegs to hold the joints in place. The foundation is usually stone, as it won't rot as will wood. The sheathing can be either vertical boards with battens to cover spaces between the boards, or horizontal clapboards.
