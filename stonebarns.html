Stone Barns

Stone barns are prevalent in the eastern United States, especially in eastern Pennsylvania and upstate New York. In Europe, they are prevalent in the British Isles, notably Ireland. Wherever the land was rocky, removing stones (truly thankless work, even with tractors) makes the land easier to farm, and the stones can be used to construct buildings. It can easily be said that stone barns are common wherever the land is rocky.
